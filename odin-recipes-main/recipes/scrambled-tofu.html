Scrambled tofu

The best one you've ever had!

Add a tablespoon of olive oil to a pan and warm it over medium heat. To crumble the tofu, I place the tofu in the pan, then mash it with a potato masher. Thatâ€™s my preferred method, but you could also simply crumble it into the pan with your hands, or mash it with a fork. Cook it, stirring frequently for 3-4 minutes

Next, add the nutritional yeast, salt, turmeric and garlic powder and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring. Pour in the non-dairy milk and stir. Serve immediately!
